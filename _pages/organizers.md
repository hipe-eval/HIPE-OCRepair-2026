## HIPE-OCRepair 2026 Organizing Team

The competition is organized by an interdisciplinary team with expertise in natural language processing, digital humanities, historical text mining, and shared task coordination.

In alphabetical order:

- _Emanuela Boros_ — EPFL, Switzerland  
  Postdoctoral researcher working on OCR post-correction and information extraction in historical sources.

- _Simon Clematide_ — University of Zurich, Switzerland  
  Initiator of the HIPE evaluation series, experienced in multilingual NLP, named entity processing, and evaluation campaign organization.

- _Maud Ehrmann_ — EPFL, Switzerland  
  Initiator of the HIPE evaluation series; expert in information extraction, historical NLP, and digital humanities.

- _Andrianos Michail_ — University of Zurich, Switzerland  
  PhD student with top-performing shared task submissions and extensive mentoring experience.

- _Juri Opitz_ — University of Zurich, Switzerland

- _Florian Wagner_ — University of Zurich, Switzerland
