## HIPE-OCRepair 2026 Organizing Team

The HIPE-2026 lab is organized by an interdisciplinary team with expertise in natural language processing, digital humanities, historical text mining, and shared task coordination. In alphabetical order:

- Emanuela Boros — EPFL, Switzerland  
  Postdoctoral researcher working on OCR post-correction and information extraction in historical sources.

- Simon Clematide — University of Zurich, Switzerland  
  Initiator of the HIPE evaluation series, experienced in multilingual NLP, named entity processing, and evaluation campaign organization.

- Maud Ehrmann — EPFL, Switzerland  
  Initiator of the HIPE evaluation series; expert in information extraction, historical NLP, and digital humanities.

- Andrianos Michail — University of Zurich, Switzerland  
  PhD researcher with top-performing shared task submissions and extensive mentoring experience.

- Juri Opitz — University of Zurich, Switzerland  
  Postdoctoral researcher with a focus on semantic representation, evaluation, and generative models.

- Florian Wagner — University of Zurich, Switzerland
  Bachelor student in computational linguistics with experience in data curation and annotation for historical text mining.

## Partner Projects and Data Providers

- [Impresso — _Media Monitoring of the Past_](https://impresso-project.ch)  
  A research initiative dedicated to large-scale semantic enrichment of digitized historical newspapers.
